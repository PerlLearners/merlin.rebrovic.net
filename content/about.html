Professionally -------------- I'm currently an interaction designer and a front-end developer in [Five Minutes][1]. Web and Mobile is where I fight for better use of technology and great user experiences. I also co-founded a small web and mobile service company [Tardigrada][2] a few years ago. It's a bit quieter now, but still alive. While acquiring my computer science degree, what now seems a long time ago, I started working as a back-end developer. After some time I gravitated towards front-end and really liked it. Design is very close to front-end so I managed to mix my love for drawing and psychology with user interface design. Having worked on a lot of projects, I had to think about product development and user experience, and that puts me where I am today. Privately --------- The line between private and professional life is pretty blurred for me because I really love what I do. That's why my private time is also filled with technology and design. On those occasions when I manage to separate myself from the screen I usually hang out with friends and family. I love exercising and being outdoors. Over the course of my life I've tried different ball and racquet sports, swimming, weight training, running, cycling, ice and roller skating, hiking, orienteering, spelunking (caving), dancing, archery, white water rafting and parachuting.
