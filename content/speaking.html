Webtanken

Conference, 2013-11-28, Kristiansand, Norway

Title: Mobile Commerce—Technical Overview Smartphones became essential part of our lives even though they went mainstream only a few years ago. The consequence is that we're still learning how people use mobile technology and especially how they approach mobile shopping. There are many different technologies to build your m-commerce solution upon, each with specific pros and cons. In this talk I presented what technology to choose in a particular situation and how each solution empowers customers and improves online store business.

Windays 13

Conference, 2013-04-24, Umag, Croatia

Title: Building Modern Software: User Experience and Design Do you remember the days when all that was needed was to be first or have the most features? They are over. Users have become much smarter and more demanding. The only way to be successful is to think of design being as important as engineering. This talk described how designer and developer roles intertwine from the beginning and how they should have user's goals in mind during the whole project. * [SlideShare presentation](http://www.slideshare.net/merlinrebrovic/building-modern-software-ux-and-design) * [Blog post]([[ blog/windays-2013.html ]])

With a computer science degree and a few years of working experience in the field, he shifted to design realizing that you need both design and engineering to make great products. When not building something new, Merlin can be found reading or relaxing in nature.
