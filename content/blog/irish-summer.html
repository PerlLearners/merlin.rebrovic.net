I remarked on the weather to my taxi driver and he had the best reply ever. --- My third visit to Dublin was also windy and rainy; no surprises there even in late July. I remarked on the weather to my taxi driver and he said:

Do you know when Noah had to build an ark because God ordered him to do so? After Noah boarded the ark with his family and all animals, it rained for forty days and forty nights.

Best summer we ever had.
