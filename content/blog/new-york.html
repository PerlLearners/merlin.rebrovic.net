--- title: New York created: !!timestamp '2013-07-20' category: Travel --- {{ macros.external_image("2013-07-20_midtown_ny.jpg", "A view towards Midtown.") }} I've been in New York for only a few days and it already made a certain impression. It's colourful, diverse, busy, dangerous and loud. It never sleeps. It's a concrete jungle in every sense of the word. And like a real jungle, it's full of life below the ground, on it, and high above.
