--- title: Tablet desk lamp created: !!timestamp '2013-01-29' category: Design --- The hotel room I'm currently staying in has terrible lighting; it's almost impossible to read from paper. The work desk in a corner is even darker. It would be impossible to use it as a drawing surface, so I devised a little improvised desk lamp. {{ macros.external_image("2013-01-29_tablet_desk_lamp.jpg", "A tablet as a desk lamp.") }} The app I'm using on my Nexus 10 is [Galaxy Nexus Flashlight][1]. It's really simple and elegant, but the selling point for me was that it can be used with the screen turned off. Try it out, it's nice. [1]: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nionsoftware.flashlight
