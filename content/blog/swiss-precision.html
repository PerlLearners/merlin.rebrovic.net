--- title: Swiss precision created: !!timestamp '2014-07-04' category: Travel --- {{ macros.external_image("2014-07-04_ticket.jpg", "Image description.") }} Swiss precision is world famous, but sometimes it works against you. A new car and an unknown road can easily get you distracted, making you speed a bit as a consequence. Cameras and radars are always watching, so they caught me in the exact situation. I was driving 57 km/h in a 50 km/h area. They had deducted 3 km/h as a safety margin and then mailed me a ticket for 4 km/h I was over the limit. There are almost no reckless drivers in Switzerland and now you know why.
