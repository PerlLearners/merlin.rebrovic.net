Boring lectures were too much to bear. --- {{ macros.external_image("2016-04-13_postit-doodles.jpg", "A bunch of doodles on Post-It notes.") }} Yesterday [I tweeted that I doodled something after a long time][1]. It struck me this morning that I became proficient in drawing while I was in school. There weren't any drawing classes there, but the sheer amount of boring lectures was too much for a curious mind. My escape from that torture was filling any white space with doodles, comics, and sometimes, full page drawings. [1]: https://twitter.com/merlinrebrovic/status/719924868326801408
