But when I came closer and saw how many things are not finished, and that a thriving fast-track business had been build around it with thousands of tourists visiting every day, the castle lost some of its charm. It, as many other things in life, is better observed from far away where its external beauty shines. It is, after all, the most beautiful fairy tale castle.
